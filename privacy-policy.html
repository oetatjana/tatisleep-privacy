Privacy Policy

Tati Sleep  ·  Last updated: June 28, 2026

✅  Tati Sleep does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. All information stays on your device.

1. About this app

Tati Sleep is a baby sleep music application that plays lullabies, white noise, and nature sounds to help infants and toddlers sleep. It includes a sleep timer and a local sleep log for parents to track sessions.

2. Data we collect

We collect no personal data. The only information the app stores is:

  • Sleep session logs — dates, durations, track names, and optional notes you enter. These are stored exclusively on your device and never transmitted anywhere.
  • Premium unlock status — a local flag indicating whether you have purchased the premium upgrade. This is stored on your device only.

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information.

3. In-app purchases

Tati Sleep offers a one-time premium unlock purchase processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details. Google's handling of payment data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA)

Tati Sleep is designed for use by parents and caregivers of infants and young children. The app itself does not collect any information from children or adults. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

No data — from children or adults — is ever collected, stored on external servers, or shared with third parties.

5. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services solely for core functionality:

  • Google Play Billing — to process the optional one-time purchase. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or social media platforms.

6. Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

7. Data retention & deletion

All data (sleep logs, premium status) is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All" button in the Sleep Log screen, or
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data.

8. Security

Because no data leaves your device, there is no server-side storage or transmission to secure. Your sleep logs are only as accessible as your device itself.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top. We encourage you to review it periodically.
